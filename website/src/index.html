Questions

Questions, answered

Can I keep using Personal for free?

Yes, forever. Personal gives you unlimited agents and every feature for a single player, with no credit card and no time limit.

Who pays for a team?

The team owner. Everyone you invite rides on the owner's plan, so members never enter a card of their own.

What counts as a seat?

One seat is one member who accepts your invite. Pending invites are free, and seats are prorated when people join or leave.

What happens when my trial ends?

Nothing is deleted. Your Space drops back to the free Personal plan and every agent and mission stays put. Add a card whenever you are ready to continue as a team.

How do I move an agent into a team?

Open the agent and share it with your Space. It keeps its history and skills, and your teammates can pick up any mission from where it stands.

Do members need to pay or download anything?

Members just accept the invite and download the free app. Billing stays with the owner.
